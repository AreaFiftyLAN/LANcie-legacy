Area FiftyLAN

Area FiftyLAN is the hottest new gaming event of the TU Delft. On this spectacular all-weekend event, all students are welcome to play their favorite games with their fellow students and friends. You decide what to play! There will be exciting competitive tournaments like FIFA, League of Legends and Trackmania. If you're not into competitive gaming however, no problem! You can play all the games you want, whether that's The Sims, Age of Empires 2, Rollercoaster Tycoon or Minecraft, you decide!

That is not all! There will be plenty to do offline as well. Poker and foosball masters can also show off in this event. In the Launch Area, you'll find pinball machines, airhockey tables, table football tables and various retro gaming consoles. An overview of things you can do besides gaming can be found below.

A place to sleep and food for the whole weekend will be arranged. Area FiftyLAN will be an unforgettable event you do not want to miss! Are you ready for the launch?
